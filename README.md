# 🗂️ Source Card

> **"It fits inside an image. It articulates one idea. And it's designed to be shared as an image."**

Source Card is a minimalist web tool that turns your thoughts into "pure ideas." No links, no bios, no "check out my newsletter." Just a clean, shareable PNG designed to spread across the internet on its own merit.

---

### 📥 The Workflow
1. **Type:** Drop a singular, potent idea into the editor.
2. **Bake:** The site renders your text into a perfectly formatted, high-resolution PNG.
3. **Release:** Download it and throw it into the wild.

---

### 🧠 The Philosophy
Most platforms are designed to build "brands." They want you to link back to a profile, a Substack, or a portfolio. Source Card is the opposite. 

* **No Source:** The idea is the star, not the person who typed it.
* **No Friction:** People shouldn't have to "click" to understand the value. 
* **High Velocity:** Images move faster than articles. Memes move faster than manifestos.

### 🛠️ Technical Implementation
This is a lightweight frontend application that "bakes" text into pixels.
* **Canvas API:** Used to render text dynamically into a 1200x630 (or square) container.
* **Client-Side Generation:** We don't store your ideas on a server. They are generated in your browser and belong to the internet the moment you download them.
* **Zero Bloat:** No tracking, no cookies, no logins.

---

### 🎨 Customization (The "Remix" Spirit)
While the design is intentionally minimal, the code allows for:
* **Dynamic Typography:** Type scales automatically to fit the card.
* **Export:** Instant `.png` generation for Twitter, Discord, and Instagram.
